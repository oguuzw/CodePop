## How It Works

1. When the page loads, an animated gradient background starts
2. A "click anywhere" message blinks at the bottom
3. When you click anywhere on the screen:
   - A programming language logo appears at the clicked point
   - The logo disappears after 1 second
   - The next click displays the next logo in sequence
